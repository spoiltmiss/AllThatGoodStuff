Difficulty: Easy
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Serves 8-10

INGREDIENTS:
100 grams Pascalls marshmallows
300 millilitres cream
500 grams Fresh'n Fruity vanilla bean yoghurt
85 grams Gregg's raspberry jelly crystals
1 Cadbury Flake (crushed)
100 grams fresh raspberries (optional)

METHOD
1. Whip cream until it forms stiff peaks
2. Add yoghurt, jelly crystals and 3/4 of the crumbled Flake. Whip until fully combined
3. Cut marshmallows in half and stir into the mixture along with the raspberries
4. Pour into a serving dish, sprinkle remaining Flake over the top and enjoy
