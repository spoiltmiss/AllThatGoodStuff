Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Serves 6-8

INGREDIENTS
For the cake:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
50 grams butter, melted
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup milk
For the topping:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1 1/2 cups boiling water

METHOD
For the cake:
1. Preheat oven to 180'C and grease a 25x20cm (or similar) baking dish
2. In a medium sized bowl, sift or whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, cocoa powder and salt. Set aside
3. In a small bowl or jug, whisk together melted butter, egg, vanilla and milk
4. Pour wet mix into the dry ingredients and fold together until just combined
5. Pour the cake batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth out the top
For the topping:
6. In a small bowl, whisk together the brown sugar and cocoa powder and sprinkle evenly over the cake mixture.
7. Carefully pour over the boiling water and bake for 30 minutes
8. Remove from the oven and serve immediately with ice cream or whipped cream
