Difficulty: Medium
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 50 minutes
Serves 12

INGREDIENTS
For the cake:
85 grams butter
1 1/2 cups flour + extra
225 grams dates, chopped
1 cup strong black coffee, boiling hot
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
For the sauce:
100 grams butter
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 1/2 cups cream

METHOD
For the cake:
1. Soak chopped dates in hot coffee for 15 minutes, then stir in the baking powder
2. Grease 20cm baking dish and coat with extra flour
3. Sift flour, baking powder and salt in large bowl. Whisk until completely combined
4. Cream butter and sugar (beat together until pale)
5. Beat eggs (one at a time) into creamed butter
6. Add half of the flour mixture and beat on low until just combined
7. Add the date mixture to the above along with the remaining flour mix and stir until just combined
8. Pour into baking dish and bake for 35-40 minutes or until a knife inserted comes out clean
For the sauce:
9. While the cake is baking, add all sauce ingredients to a saucepan bring to the boil
10. Reduce heat to medium and simmer, stirring often until sauce darkens slightly and thickens
Assembly:
11. Once cake is ready, poke holes all through it and pour over half of the caramel sauce
12. Serve with ice cream and remaining caramel sauce
