Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 30 minutes
Cooking time: 20-25 minutes
Serves 8-16

INGREDIENTS
For the cake:
1 1/2 cups flour
3/4 cup cocoa
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup water
1/2 cup flavourless oil
4 teaspoons white vinegar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
For the glaze:
1 cup dairy-free dark chocolate, chopped
1 cup dairy-free cream (soy or coconut)

METHOD
For the cake:
1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius
2. Grease and line two round 20cm cake tins
3. In a large bowl, sift all dry ingredients. Stir until completely mixed
4. Add all wet ingredients. Mix until fully combined and smooth
5. Split evenly between the tins and bake for approximately 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean
6. Leave to cool completely while you make your glaze
For the glaze:
7. Chop the chocolate into fine pieces, pour into a heat-proof bowl and set aside
8. Heat the cream either in a small saucepan or the microwave and remove from the heat before it boils
9. Pour heated cream over chocolate and let stand for 5 minutes
10. Stir chocolate and cream mixture until completely smooth.
11. Refrigerate 1/3 of glaze for 15 mins
Assembly:
13. Place one layer of the cake onto a large plate and spread the chilled glaze over the top
14. Place second layer on top, pour over the remaining glaze and serve
