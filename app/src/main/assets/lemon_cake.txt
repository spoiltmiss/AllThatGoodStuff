Difficulty: Easy
Prep time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 40 minutes
Serves 12-16

INGREDIENTS
For the cake:
150g pottle Fresh'n Fruity Dreamy Lemon yoghurt
1 pottle flavourless oil
2 pottles caster sugar
3 pottles plain flour
2 tsp baking powder
finely grated zest of 2 lemons
3 eggs
For the glaze:
1/2 pottle icing sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice

METHOD
1. Preheat oven to 170ËšC fanbake.
2. Grease the sides of a 20cm springform cake tin and line the base with baking paper
3. Combine all ingredients except icing sugar and lemon juice in a large bowl, scraping out the pottle with a spatula between ingredients
4. Mix until evenly combined. Transfer to prepared tin and bake until the top is golden and a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean
5. Allow to cool in the tin, then transfer to a serving plate
For the glaze:
6. Half-fill the pottle with icing sugar then add lemon juice and stir until smooth, adding a little extra if necessary to make a runny glaze
7. Drizzle over the cake and garnish with fresh seasonal berries
